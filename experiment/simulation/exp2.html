Merging and Splitting of a Poisson Process

Instructions
  • Two independent Poisson emitters operate at rates \(\lambda_1\) and \(\lambda_2\).
  • Merge: Combines both streams into one with rate \(\lambda_1 + \lambda_2\). The moment of merging is marked on the line chart.
  • Adjust \(\lambda_1\) and \(\lambda_2\) using the sliders. Click “Merge” to see the effect, and "Reset & Split" to revert.
  • Left Panel: Visualizes emitters merging and splitting.
  • Right Panel: Two real-time charts:
    1. Live line chart: emissions per second.
    2. Histogram: empirical probability of emission counts vs the theoretical PMF.
  • Observations Panel: Tracks the running average emission rate vs the theoretical rate \(\lambda\).
