Emergence of Poisson Process from Bernoulli Process

Instructions
  • This experiment visualizes the convergence of the Binomial distribution to the Poisson distribution for \( p = \lambda/n \) as \( n \to \infty \).
  • Use the sliders below to choose:
    1. \(n\): number of Bernoulli trials
    2. \(\lambda\): Poisson mean
  • For the selected \((n, \lambda)\):
    1. Plot Binomial PMF
    2. Plot Poisson PMF
    3. Compare visually and via average absolute error
  • As \(n\) grows large, observe the distributions align and error reduces.
