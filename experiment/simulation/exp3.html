Properties of Inter-arrival Times

Instructions
  • This experiment demonstrates two key properties: that inter-arrival times follow an **Exponential distribution** and that they are **independent** of each other.
  • Use the slider to set the Poisson rate (\(\lambda\)) and press **Start**.
  • The **Distribution Analysis** plot shows the histogram of waiting times. Observe how it matches the theoretical Exponential PDF (orange line).
  • The **Independence Analysis** plot shows a scatter plot of each waiting time \(T_i\) against the next one \(T_{i+1}\). A random cloud of points with a flat best-fit line (slope near 0) indicates independence.
